Shiftable

Making request shift changes easier, simpler, and more convenient than ever before!

Shiftable was made as a group project built on Ruby on Rails & JavaScript. Shiftable provides a reliable, convenient, and simple platform for employees and shift-managers to manage, share and edit shift schedules. It uses full-calendar JavaScript library, and draggable/droppable library to make an easy to use calendar for shift managers and employees.

Users can log in, admin can assign work shifts to employees, employees can request shift change and admin has the ability to approve or reject requests.

Fitbud

A social media app designed to help you stay fit and meet new people at the same time.

Fitbud is an application that makes it easy for users to find others interested in their favorite outdoor activity. Finding the motivation to workout alone can be difficult, but with the help of FitBud, you can make a new friend to help you stay motivated and on track with your goals!
Fitbud was an individual Rails project for the August 2014 Web Development Immersive Course at General Assembly DC.

Users can sign up, pick favorite activity, and find and message others who also chose a similar activity.
